About my life!

I was born in Brazil and lived there until I was 17 years old.
I was studying Mechanical Engineering at the time and moved
to Italy to continue my studies. Living in Milan, I rediscovered
my passion for drawing and art, and now I'm at my second year
of Communication Design at Politecnico di Milano.

Curiosities about myself

I really love sports, and the list of the one's I've practised is
quite long: about 6 years of competitive swimming, 8 years of volleyball,
2 years of Taekwondo and many others like basketball, athletics, gymnastics,
cycling, soccer and futsal. Today I don't have much time, but I've been going to
the gym regularly for almost 7 years. My favorite soccer team is São Paulo FC.

I always loved drawing, but I hadn't thought about taking it seriously
until I was almost 19 years old! I used to draw random things, but then
I started to draw the animes I loved when I was a kid, especially
Dragon Ball, and just became in love with drawing manga style!

I love cinema, series, and anime. The movies I've watched
the most might be the Lord of the Rings trilogy. My favorite series
are Breaking Bad and Daredevil! The most nostalgic animes to me are
Dragon Ball and Dragon Ball Z. These, along with many others like Pokémon,
Digimon and Yu-Gi-Oh!, remind me of happy mornings at my grandmother' house.

I learned to read by reading a famous children comic in Brazil called
"Turma da Mônica", which I love to this day.
My favorite authors are Machado de Assis, Jorge Amado, Oscar Wilde,
Sir Arthur Conan Doyle and Charles Dickens.
I'm also passionate about mangas, and I have a collection with about 14 titles.
My favorite one recently is a fairly new one, called "The Promised Neverland".
